1. Chemical Pollution

• Algal Blooms (Harmful Algae that Damage Ecosystems)

• Microplastics & Organic Waste

3. Physical Parameters

• Water Temperature (Affects Aquatic Life)

• Turbidity (Cloudiness of Water due to Sediments)
